Project Description

JobAid is a data science project based on data from glassdoor.com.

This project presents various interesting analysis that are helpful for preparing for a job, such as programing words that are most frequently mentioned during interviews; most critical job requirements for a certain job across companies; geographical reports of quantity demands for certain jobs, etc
