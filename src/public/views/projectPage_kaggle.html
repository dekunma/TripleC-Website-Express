What about housing price

Our kaggle project is meant to extract information from the database on kaggle Housing price in Beijing, which originally collected from LianJia.com a Chinese online real estate marketplace, and analyze the potential trends and behaviour patterns of the data in order to predict the housing price in Beijing. Throughout the analysis, we will apply hypothesis testing, data visualization and machine learning models etc. techniques to generate our final report.
