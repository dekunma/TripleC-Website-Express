Project Description

Meetful is a website to create group meetings, record availabilities of group members, and provide optimal meeting times for group events. It has a clean interface to choose availabilities, and would show optimal time slots for meeting in addition to all group members’ availabilities.

Members can use unique usernames and optional passwords to sign in the corresponding event.
